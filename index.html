NASA Space Biology Research Areas

Animal Biology

Understanding how spaceflight affects model organisms like rodents and nematodes, examining metabolism, growth, and physiological adaptation to microgravity.

Plant Biology

Research on plant growth, development, and metabolism in space, enabling sustainable food production for long-duration missions.

Microbiology

Studying microbial communities in spacecraft, including changes in virulence, drug resistance, and their roles in life support systems.

Cell & Molecular Biology

Investigating how spaceflight alters gene expression, protein function, and cellular communication at the most fundamental levels :cite[8].
